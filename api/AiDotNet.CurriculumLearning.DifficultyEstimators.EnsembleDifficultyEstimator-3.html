Remarks

For Beginners: This estimator combines multiple difficulty estimators into a single, more robust estimate. Different estimators may capture different aspects of difficulty, and combining them can provide a more comprehensive view.

Combination Strategies:

  • Weighted Average: Sum of (weight Ã— difficulty) for each estimator
  • Maximum: Highest difficulty from any estimator
  • Minimum: Lowest difficulty from any estimator
  • Median: Middle value of all difficulties
  • Rank Average: Average of per-estimator difficulty ranks

Benefits:

  • More robust to individual estimator failures
  • Captures multiple aspects of sample difficulty
  • Can combine model-based and expert-defined estimates
