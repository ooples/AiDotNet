Remarks

For Beginners: Diversity strategies select samples that are diverse or representative of the data distribution. This helps ensure the model learns from a variety of examples rather than similar ones.

Common Diversity Strategies:

  • K-Center/CoreSet: Selects samples that maximize coverage of the feature space
  • K-Means: Clusters samples and selects from each cluster
  • Random: Simple random sampling as a baseline
  • Density-Weighted: Samples from high-density regions
