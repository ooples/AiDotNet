Remarks

For Beginners: Time series models are specialized algorithms that analyze data collected over time (like daily temperatures, monthly sales, or yearly population) to identify patterns and make predictions about future values.

This factory helps you create different types of time series models without needing to know their internal implementation details. Think of it like ordering a specific tool from a catalog - you just specify what you need, and the factory provides it.
