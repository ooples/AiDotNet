Remarks

STOI predicts the intelligibility of degraded speech signals. It correlates well with human listening tests and is commonly used to evaluate speech enhancement and separation.

Values range from 0 to 1, where higher values indicate better intelligibility. - >0.9: Excellent intelligibility - 0.7-0.9: Good intelligibility - 0.5-0.7: Fair intelligibility - <0.5: Poor intelligibility

Based on "A short-time objective intelligibility measure for time-frequency weighted noisy speech" by Taal et al. (2011).
