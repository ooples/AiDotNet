Remarks

For Beginners: A matrix is like a table or grid of numbers arranged in rows and columns. You can perform various mathematical operations on matrices such as addition, subtraction, and multiplication. Matrices are commonly used in AI for storing and manipulating data, like representing weights in neural networks.
