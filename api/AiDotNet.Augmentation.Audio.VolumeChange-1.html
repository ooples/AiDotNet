Remarks

For Beginners: Volume change augmentation makes audio louder or quieter. This helps models become robust to different recording volumes and microphone distances.

Gain in dB:

  • +6 dB ≈ 2x louder
  • 0 dB = no change
  • -6 dB ≈ 0.5x volume
