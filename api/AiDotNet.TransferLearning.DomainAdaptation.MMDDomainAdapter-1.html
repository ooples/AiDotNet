Remarks

For Beginners: Maximum Mean Discrepancy (MMD) is a way to measure how different two distributions are. Think of it like comparing the "average characteristics" of two groups. This adapter minimizes the difference between the average properties of source and target data.

Imagine you have photos from two different cameras. MMD would measure how different the "average photo" from each camera is, and then adjust them to have similar average properties.
