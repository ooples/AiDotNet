Remarks

Performance Targets:

  • AMD RX 7900 XTX: ~25,000 GFLOPS (FP32)
  • Intel Arc A770: ~18,000 GFLOPS (FP32)
  • NVIDIA RTX 4090: ~30,000 GFLOPS (OpenCL, ~82,000 via CUDA)

Why OpenCL:

  • Cross-vendor: Works on AMD, Intel, NVIDIA, Apple, ARM Mali, Qualcomm Adreno
  • No vendor lock-in: Not tied to NVIDIA's ecosystem
  • Mature ecosystem: CLBlast, clBLAS provide cuBLAS-like performance
