Remarks

For Beginners: This is the simplest batch selection strategy. It ranks samples by their informativeness scores and selects the top-k, optionally filtering out samples that are too similar to already-selected ones.

How It Works:

  1. Rank all candidates by informativeness score
  2. Select top candidate
  3. Filter remaining candidates by diversity threshold
  4. Repeat until batch is complete

Trade-offs:

  • Simple and fast
  • May miss globally diverse points
  • Works well when top candidates are naturally diverse
