Remarks

CLBlast provides cuBLAS-level performance on AMD/Intel GPUs.

Benchmark results on AMD gfx1012 (RX 5300/5500):

  • 4096x4096 GEMM: 2,532 GFLOPS (vs 514 GFLOPS with our kernel)
  • This is 4.9x faster than our hand-written OpenCL kernel
