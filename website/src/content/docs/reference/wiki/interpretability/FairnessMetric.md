## Fields

| Field | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `DemographicParity` | Demographic parity: equal positive prediction rates across groups. |
| `DisparateImpact` | Disparate impact: ratio of positive prediction rates between groups. |
| `EqualOpportunity` | Equal opportunity: equal true positive rates across groups. |
| `EqualizedOdds` | Equalized odds: equal true positive and false positive rates across groups. |
| `PredictiveParity` | Predictive parity: equal precision across groups. |
| `StatisticalParityDifference` | Statistical parity difference: difference in positive prediction rates. |
