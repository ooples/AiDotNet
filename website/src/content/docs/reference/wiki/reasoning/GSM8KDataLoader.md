## How It Works

GSM8K (Grade School Math 8K) dataset format:
{
"question": "Natalia sold clips to 48 of her friends in April...",
"answer": "She sells 48/2 = 24 clips in May.\nShe sells 24/2 = 12... #### 12"
}
