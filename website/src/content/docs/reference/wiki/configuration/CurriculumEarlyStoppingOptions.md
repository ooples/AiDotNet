`Options & Configuration` · `AiDotNet.Configuration`

Early stopping options for curriculum learning.

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `Enabled` | Gets or sets whether early stopping is enabled. |
| `MinDelta` | Gets or sets the minimum improvement required to reset patience counter. |
| `Patience` | Gets or sets the patience (epochs without improvement before stopping). |
