WellSaid Labs: enterprise neural TTS with custom voice avatars for brand consistency.

## For Beginners

WellSaid Labs specializes in enterprise neural TTS with custom
voice avatars that maintain brand consistency across all content. Their models focus on
natural prosody and expressiveness, making them popular for corporate training,
marketing, and internal communications. This local implementation provides offline inference.

## Methods

| Method | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `PreprocessText(String)` | Converts text to normalized character embeddings (char/128.0). |
| `Synthesize(String)` | Synthesizes speech using WellSaidLabs's API-compatible local inference pipeline. |
