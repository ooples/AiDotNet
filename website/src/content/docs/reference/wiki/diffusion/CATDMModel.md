`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Diffusion.VirtualTryOn`

CATDM model for controllable appearance transfer diffusion for virtual try-on.

## For Beginners

CATDM gives you more control over how clothes fit. Beyond just
placing clothes on a person, you can control how tight or loose they look, how the
fabric drapes, and other fine details of the garment's appearance.

## How It Works

CATDM uses appearance-transfer attention (ATA) to transfer garment appearance features
while a shape control module ensures proper garment deformation based on body pose.
Supports fine-grained attribute control like fit tightness and drape.
