`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Diffusion.Panorama`

SpotDiffusion model for spatially-organized text-guided panorama generation.

## For Beginners

SpotDiffusion lets you create panoramas where different parts
show different things — like a beach on the left transitioning to mountains on the right.
You describe each section separately and it blends them smoothly.

## How It Works

SpotDiffusion enables spatial layout control in panoramic generation by assigning
different text prompts to different spatial regions. Each region is independently
guided while seamless blending is maintained in overlap zones.
