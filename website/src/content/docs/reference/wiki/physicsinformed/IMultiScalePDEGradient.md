`Interfaces` · `AiDotNet.PhysicsInformed.Interfaces`

Provides gradient information for multi-scale PDE residuals.

## Methods

| Method | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `ComputeScaleCouplingGradient(Int32,Int32,[],[],[],PDEDerivatives<>,PDEDerivatives<>)` | Computes gradients of the coupling term. |
| `ComputeScaleResidualGradient(Int32,[],[],PDEDerivatives<>)` | Computes gradients of the scale residual with respect to outputs and derivatives. |
