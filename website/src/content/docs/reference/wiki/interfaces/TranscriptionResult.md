`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Interfaces`

Represents the result of a transcription operation.

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `Confidence` | Gets or sets the confidence score for the transcription (0.0 to 1.0). |
| `DurationSeconds` | Gets or sets the total duration of the audio in seconds. |
| `Language` | Gets or sets the detected or specified language. |
| `Segments` | Gets or sets the word-level segments with timestamps. |
| `Text` | Gets or sets the transcribed text. |
