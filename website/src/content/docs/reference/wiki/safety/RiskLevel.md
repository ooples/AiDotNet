EU AI Act risk classification levels (Articles 5, 6, 50, 52).

## For Beginners

The EU AI Act classifies AI systems into risk levels.
Unacceptable systems are banned. High-risk systems face strict requirements.
Limited-risk systems need transparency. Minimal-risk systems are largely unregulated.

## Fields

| Field | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `High` | Strict requirements (safety, transparency, human oversight). |
| `Limited` | Transparency requirements (chatbots, deepfakes). |
| `Minimal` | Largely unregulated (spam filters, video games). |
| `Unacceptable` | Banned practices (social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance). |
