`Options & Configuration` ┬À `AiDotNet.Safety.Audio`

Configuration for voice protection (anti-cloning) modules.

## For Beginners

Use this to configure voice protection strength.
Higher strength makes voice cloning harder but may be more audible.

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `SampleRate` | Target sample rate. |
| `Strength` | Protection strength (0.0-1.0). |
| `Technique` | Protection technique to use. |
