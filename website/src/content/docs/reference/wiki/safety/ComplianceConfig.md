`Options & Configuration` ┬Ę `AiDotNet.Safety.Compliance`

Configuration for regulatory compliance checking modules.

## For Beginners

Use this to configure which regulatory frameworks to check
compliance for. Enable the ones that apply to your deployment region and use case.

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `AIActRiskLevel` | The EU AI Act risk level classification. |
| `EUAIAct` | Whether to check EU AI Act compliance. |
| `GDPR` | Whether to check GDPR compliance. |
| `SOC2` | Whether to check SOC2 compliance. |
