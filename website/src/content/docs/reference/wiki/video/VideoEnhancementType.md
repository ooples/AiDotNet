`Enums` · `AiDotNet.Video.Interfaces`

Types of video enhancement operations.

## For Beginners

Each type represents a different way to improve video quality.
Some enhancers may support multiple types.

## Fields

| Field | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `Denoising` | Removes noise and grain from video. |
| `FrameInterpolation` | Increases frame rate by generating intermediate frames. |
| `GeneralEnhancement` | General quality enhancement (combination of techniques). |
| `OpticalFlow` | Estimates motion between frames (optical flow). |
| `Stabilization` | Removes camera shake and stabilizes footage. |
| `SuperResolution` | Increases video resolution (e.g., 480p to 4K). |
