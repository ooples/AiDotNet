TransMix (Chen et al., 2022) - attention-guided mixing for Vision Transformers.
Creates patch-level mixing masks based on attention-like scores.

| Method | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `ApplyTransMix(ImageTensor<>,ImageTensor<>,Vector<>,Vector<>,AugmentationContext<>)` | Mixes two images using patch-level attention-guided masks. |
