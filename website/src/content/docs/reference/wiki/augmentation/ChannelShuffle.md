`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Augmentation.Image`

Randomly shuffles the order of color channels.

## How It Works

Randomly permutes the RGB channels (e.g., RGB → BRG or GBR). This teaches models
to be robust to color channel ordering and can prevent overfitting to specific color patterns.
