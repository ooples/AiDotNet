`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Augmentation.Image`

Applies a sepia tone filter to the image.

## How It Works

Sepia creates a warm brownish tone reminiscent of vintage photographs. The transform
uses the standard sepia matrix commonly used in image processing.
