`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Data.Graph`

Thin wrapper around `MolecularDatasetLoader` for the QM9 dataset.

## How It Works

QM9 contains ~134K small organic molecules (up to 9 heavy atoms: C, H, N, O, F)
with 19 quantum mechanical properties computed with DFT.
Standard benchmark for molecular property prediction.

## Constructors

| Constructor | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `Qm9DataLoader(Qm9DataLoaderOptions)` | Initializes a new QM9 data loader. |
