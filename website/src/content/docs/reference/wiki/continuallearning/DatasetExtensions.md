Extension methods for IDataset to convert to DataPoints.

## Methods

| Method | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `GetAllDataPoints(IDataset<,,>,Int32)` | Converts a dataset to an enumerable of DataPoint objects. |
| `GetDataPointsAt(IDataset<,,>,IEnumerable<Int32>,Int32)` | Gets a subset of data points from the dataset. |
