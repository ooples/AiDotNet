Defines the aggressiveness level of stop word removal.

## Fields

| Field | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `Aggressive` | Removes most stop words including prepositions. |
| `Light` | Removes only basic articles and auxiliaries. |
| `Medium` | Removes common stop words (default). |
