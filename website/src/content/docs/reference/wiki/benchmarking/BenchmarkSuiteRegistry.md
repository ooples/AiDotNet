`Helpers & Utilities` · `AiDotNet.Benchmarking`

Registry for benchmark suite discovery and suite instantiation.

## Methods

| Method | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `GetAvailableSuites` | Lists benchmark suites available in the current build. |
| `GetDisplayName(BenchmarkSuite)` | Gets a stable display name for a suite. |
| `GetSuiteKind(BenchmarkSuite)` | Gets the suite category/kind. |
