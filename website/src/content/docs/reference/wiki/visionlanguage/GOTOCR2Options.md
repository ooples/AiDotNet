`Options & Configuration` · `AiDotNet.VisionLanguage.Document`

Configuration options for GOT-OCR2: 580M unified OCR model for text, tables, charts, equations, and music scores.

## For Beginners

These options configure the GOTOCR2 model. Default values follow the original paper settings.

## Constructors

| Constructor | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `GOTOCR2Options(GOTOCR2Options)` | Initializes a new instance by copying from another instance. |

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `EnableMathOCR` | Gets or sets whether to enable mathematical equation OCR. |
| `EnableMusicOCR` | Gets or sets whether to enable music score OCR. |
