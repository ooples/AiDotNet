`Models & Types` · `AiDotNet.Audio.MusicAnalysis`

Result of beat tracking.

## For Beginners

BeatTrackingResult provides AI safety functionality. Default values follow the original paper settings.

## Properties

| Property | Summary |
|:-----|:--------|
| `AverageBeatInterval` | Gets the average beat interval in seconds. |
| `BeatTimes` | Gets or sets the times of detected beats in seconds. |
| `ConfidenceScore` | Gets or sets the confidence score (0-1). |
| `Tempo` | Gets or sets the estimated tempo in beats per minute. |
